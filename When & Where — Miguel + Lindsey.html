Things to Know


We're sure you have a few questions, so if we haven't answered them below, don't hesitate to reach out to us! We're so happy to help with anything you may need to make this a fun and relaxing weekend.

 

  • What will the weather be like?
    • Santa Cruz is sunny and mild all year round. Expect some sun during the day and cool, breezy nights.
  • What should I wear?
    • The entire wedding, from ceremony to reception, will take place outside on the beach, right in the sand! Dress is beachy chic, but do be sure to wear comfortable shoes. No heels, ladies! It will be warm in the afternoon, but coolish during sunset with that amazing ocean breeze. 
  • Will any shade be provided?
    • Yes, absolutely. There will be a huge tented area for those needing some respite from the sun.
  • What about parking for the ceremony/reception? I hear Santa Cruz gets busy during the summer months.
    • It sure does! We recommend walking if you are close by, otherwise call a taxi or Uber to get you to the beach :) It will be the smartest choice!
  • Are there any other events planned for the weekend?
    • Everything will be super laidback and casual, but there will be few things going on that everyone is welcome to join:
      • Friday, 7/15, 7pm at Twin Lakes Beach: It's bonfire time for those who want to drop by, say hi and start the festivities in true Santa Cruz style! We'll provide the fire and some blankets, but feel free to bring anything else you may want or need! 
      • Sunday, 7/17, no time in particular: We don't know about you, but we will be getting breakfast burritos at our favorite local spot, Pleasure Point Market, just like we do every weekend. We have no idea what time this will be, but even if we're not there, do yourself a favor... order The Barrel and enjoy the best view Santa Cruz has to offer!
